# Sales performance analysis dashboard
Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard
Business Problem

The company wants to understand sales performance across different regions, product categories, and sales representatives to identify opportunities for improving revenue and profitability.

Objective

Analyze sales data to:

Identify top-performing regions
Identify profitable product categories
Measure sales representative performance
Understand sales trends over time
Find loss-making areas requiring business attention
**# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes company sales data to evaluate business performance across regions, product categories, and sales representatives.

The analysis focuses on identifying revenue drivers, profit trends, regional performance, and sales opportunities.

## Business Objective

To improve profitability by understanding:

- Sales performance by region
- Product category contribution
- Sales representative effectiveness
- Revenue and profit trends
- High-performing and underperforming segments

## Dataset Fields

- Product ID
- Sales Date
- Sales Representative
- Region
- Sales Amount
- Quantity Sold
- Product Category
- Unit Cost
- Unit Price
- Profit
